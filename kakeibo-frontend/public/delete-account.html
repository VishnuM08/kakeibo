Account Deletion Request

In accordance with Google Play Store data safety guidelines, Kakeibo provides users with complete control over their financial data.

How to delete your account:
  1. Log in to the Kakeibo App or Web Portal.
  2. Navigate to the Settings page.
  3. Tap on the secure Delete Account option under Account Management.
  4. Confirm the deletion request via the provided secure prompt.

Note: This action is permanent and will instantly erase all associated tracking history, budgets, and savings goals from our secure backend servers.

If you uninstalled the app and wish to delete your data immediately, click below to log into the web portal and perform the deletion, or directly contact developer support.
