Delete Your Account

Complete control over your Kakeibo financial data.

How to request data deletion

  1. Log in to the Kakeibo App or Web Portal.
  2. Navigate to the Settings page.
  3. Tap on Delete Account under Account Management.
  4. Follow the secure prompt to confirm deletion.
This action is permanent and will instantly erase all your history, budgets, and savings goals.

If you uninstalled the app and wish to delete your data immediately, log in via web or contact support.
